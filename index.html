The Legend of Zelda series, created by Shigeru Miyamoto and Takashi Tezuka, and developed by Nintendo, is one of the most iconic video game franchises. Since its inception, the series has captivated players with its blend of action, adventure, and puzzle-solving elements. Although the core elements have remained the same, the series has changed significantly throughout its lifespan.
